IPB Pedia: e-BHAR Palm Oil
Harvester

The e-BHAR palm oil harvester is an innovative machine developed by the Research Team of the Department of Mechanical and Biosystem Engineering, Faculty of Agricultural Technology, IPB University. This machine is designed to move up and down during the harvesting of fresh fruit bunches (FFB), making the harvesting process easier, especially for tall palm trees, and increasing the efficiency of FFB harvesting.
